NYUGo

Python, HTML, CSS, JavaScript, SQL


Coded at HackNYU. Inspired by the idea of AmazonGo, and being fed up with waiting on long lines to pay for food at dining halls. While logged in to your NYU account, simply snap a picture of the item's barcode and it will be automatically deducted from student's account.
